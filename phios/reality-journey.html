Complete Journey

From first change to continuing Reality.

Each stage has one responsibility. No stage silently rewrites the evidence, decision or history established before it.

  1. 01 Stage

    Entry

    Purpose

    Name the change that made this Reality worth examining.

    What happens

    You report what changed, when it became noticeable, what it affects and what remains unclear.

    What remains protected

    Your words remain reported experience. Missing evidence stays visible.

  2. 02 Stage

    Reconstruction

    Purpose

    Rebuild how the current situation formed before interpreting it.

    What happens

    Conditions, timing, relationships, resources, constraints and evidence gaps are organized into one bounded structure.

    What remains protected

    Reconstruction does not diagnose, predict or invent missing facts.

  3. 03 Stage

    Reading

    Purpose

    Interpret the current Runtime without promoting inference into fact.

    What happens

    Observed evidence, reported experience, interpretation and Unknown Reality are shown separately.

    What remains protected

    Every conclusion stays traceable to its supporting evidence and confidence boundary.

  4. 04 Stage

    Navigation

    Purpose

    Turn the Reading into bounded directions that can be reviewed.

    What happens

    Available directions, reasons, constraints, first actions and review conditions become explicit.

    What remains protected

    PHI OS presents directions—not commands, guarantees or professional prescriptions.

  5. 05 Stage

    Review

    Purpose

    Compare what was expected with what actually changed.

    What happens

    You record results, non-changes, surprises, constraints and the next Runtime state.

    What remains protected

    New reports append to history; they do not rewrite the earlier Reading.

  6. 06 Stage

    Memory

    Purpose

    Preserve the outcome, unresolved Reality and selected next state.

    What happens

    Authorized Runtime Memory connects the Review to a recoverable timeline and lineage.

    What remains protected

    Only the disclosed information is retained, under the applicable access and privacy controls.

  7. 07 Stage

    Continuity

    Purpose

    Choose how this Reality should continue.

    What happens

    Continue, revise, remain open or start a new Journey without erasing the source Runtime.

    What remains protected

    Transitions require an explicit user choice and preserve parent–child lineage.

Privacy & AI boundary

Assistance stays bounded. Human responsibility remains.

The Journey is designed to support orientation and continuity, not to replace evidence, consent or accountable human judgment.

01

Purpose-limited information

The public overview stores nothing. A formal Journey uses information only for the disclosed Runtime and recovery purpose.

02

AI-assisted, not AI-authoritative

AI may help organize or interpret material. Provider output cannot become observed evidence simply because a model produced it.

03

Uncertainty remains visible

Unknown Reality and missing evidence are preserved instead of being completed by confident language.

04

Not professional or emergency advice

PHI OS is not medical diagnosis, legal advice, financial recommendation or an emergency response service.
