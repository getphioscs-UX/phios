Professional Boundary

A professional may contribute interpretation or intervention, but never outside identifiable scope, competence, consent and accountability.

01

Evidence remains traceable

Professional assessment remains separate from observed evidence.

02

Access requires purpose and consent

Access must be purpose-bound and revocable.

03

Scope remains explicit

Regulated judgment remains inside the relevant professional relationship.

04

Not an emergency service

Use appropriate local emergency services when immediate help is needed.
