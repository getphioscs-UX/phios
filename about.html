01 · Why PHI OS

We do not lack intelligence. We lack continuity.

Reality does not arrive as one question with one answer.

A transition may involve identity, relationships, resources, timing, health, responsibility and future direction at the same time.

Knowledge is divided into disciplines. Reality is not. PHI OS therefore begins by preserving the evolving context that gives any answer its meaning.

02 · Research Foundation

Reality Navigation is the category. Runtime is the continuity layer.

The Reality Navigation Thesis asks how evolving reality can become observable, computable and navigable without collapsing evidence, interpretation and uncertainty.

The Unified Runtime Framework provides the theoretical language for evidence, state, transition, memory and continuity. PHI OS is the integrated reference implementation.

Founded by Teresa Lee, the work emerged from a long effort to understand why different systems can each reveal something meaningful while still failing to preserve the whole reality across time.
